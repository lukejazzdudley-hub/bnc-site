Cadence Privacy Policy

Effective 4 July 2026 · Brand Name Changes

What we store

With an account: your email address, lyrics, song structure and cover images — synced so your songs follow you across devices. Language-pack preferences sync too.

What never leaves your phone

Audio recordings are stored only on your device. We cannot access them.

What we don't do

No ads, no selling of data, no third-party tracking. Payments are processed by Apple or Google; we never see card details.

Deletion

Delete your account in Settings → Account, or email hello@brandnamechanges.com — all synced data is removed.
