Delete your account & data

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains how to permanently delete your Cadence account and all associated data. Cadence is published by Brand Name Changes Ltd.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open Cadence and make sure you are signed in.
  2. Tap the Settings (gear) icon.
  3. Scroll to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete account, then confirm.

Your account and all associated cloud data are removed permanently and immediately, and cannot be recovered.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email hello@brandnamechanges.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Delete my account." We verify the request and delete your data within 30 days.

What data is deleted

  • Your account and email address.
  • Your lyrics, song titles and song structure.
  • Your uploaded cover images.
  • Your subscription / entitlement records held by us.

Voice recordings never leave your device, so nothing is stored on our servers to delete — removing the app deletes them from your phone.

What data is kept, and for how long

We do not retain your personal content after deletion. We may keep minimal transaction records (for example a purchase receipt) where required by law or by the app stores (Apple and Google) for tax and accounting purposes, for up to the legally required retention period. These records contain no lyrics or song content. Backups are purged on a rolling 30-day cycle.
