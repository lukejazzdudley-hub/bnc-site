Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This policy explains what Cadence (the "app") and Brand Name Changes Ltd (the data controller) collect, why, and your rights over it. It covers the app and this website.

What we collect

When you create an account we collect your email address. Your songs — titles, lyrics, structure/formatting, BPM, folder organisation, chosen username, and any cover images you upload — are synced to our servers so they appear on all your devices. We also collect basic diagnostic and usage data (app version, device type, crash logs, and in-app events such as sign-ups, trials and purchases) to operate, secure and improve the service.

What we never collect

Audio recordings you make in Cadence stay on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers. We do not access your contacts, photos (beyond an image you explicitly pick as cover art), microphone (beyond a recording you start), or location.

How we use your data

To provide and secure the service (syncing your songs, managing your account and subscription), to communicate with you about your account, and to understand and improve how Cadence is used. We do not sell your personal data.

Advertising & attribution

We advertise Cadence on platforms such as Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google and TikTok. To measure whether those ads work, our apps and website may use advertising and analytics technologies (for example the Meta pixel/SDK, Google and TikTok attribution, and mobile measurement partners) that process device and advertising identifiers and in-app events (install, trial, purchase). Where this involves tracking you across apps or websites owned by other companies, we ask permission first via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt on iOS and honour your choice; on other platforms you can opt out via your device or platform settings. We do not sell your personal data, and we share only the limited data needed to measure and optimise our own advertising.

Where your data is stored

Account and song data is stored securely with Supabase (supabase.com); cover images are stored in Supabase Storage with the same protections. Data may be processed in the EU, the UK and the US by our processors. We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.

Third-party services

Supabase (data storage & authentication), RevenueCat (subscription management), Apple and Google (Sign in with Apple / Google), Resend (transactional email), and the advertising/attribution partners named above. Each processes data only as needed, under their own privacy terms and GDPR-compliant agreements.

Your rights

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings › Profile › Delete account, right in the app. Depending on where you live (including the EU/UK under GDPR and California under the CCPA/CPRA) you may also request access to, correction of, portability of, or deletion of your personal data, and object to certain processing — by emailing support@brandnamechanges.com. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

Children

Cadence is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their personal data. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes & contact

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new date. Questions or requests: support@brandnamechanges.com.
